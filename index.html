Hour-by-Hour: Day 1

9:00 AM – 10:00 AM
Pick your niche using the Niche Finder tab. Write your product title and one sentence describing what it helps people do.
10:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Open Canva. Search "ebook template." Build your 8–15 page PDF: Cover, Problem, Steps, Tools, Action Plan.
1:00 PM – 1:30 PM
Lunch break. Step away. You deserve it.
1:30 PM – 2:30 PM
Export your PDF. Create a 1280x720 cover image in Canva. Go to gumroad.com and upload your product.
2:30 PM – 3:30 PM
Write your product description: one sentence on the problem, three bullet points on what they get, and your price.
3:30 PM – 4:00 PM
Add your Gumroad link to your bio. Publish. You are officially open for business. 🎉

Hour-by-Hour: Day 2

9:00 AM – 11:00 AM
Open My Content tab. Pick your 5 scripts. Record all 5 back-to-back. First take is fine.
11:00 AM – 12:00 PM
Edit in CapCut (free app). Add auto-captions. Add "Link in bio" text overlay to each video.
12:00 PM → 9:00 PM
Post videos at 12pm, 3pm, 6pm, 7pm, and 9pm. Reply to every comment between posts.
